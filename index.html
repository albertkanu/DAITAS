Ethonyms

"Documenting africans in Trans-Atlantic Slavery"(DATAS) develops an innovative method to examine african enthonyms from the era of the trans_atlantic trad, circa 1500-1867.The project centers on the need to understand the origins and trajectories of people of Africa descent who populated the Trans-Atlantic world in the mordern tools for analyzing social differentiation that can result in inequalities and racism. The project examine concerpt that require rethinking entrenched assumption about data justice, research, transparency and the agency of populations.
